Exhibition "Approximation Theory"

There will be an exhibition opening will take place on Wednesday afternoon in the TU Berlin Math Library of the Institut für Mathematick of the Technische Universität Berlin. This will be preceed by an introductory talk by Héctor Rodríguez. More information in the venue page.

The art-research project Approximation Theory is a collaboration between Felipe Cucker and Héctor Rodríguez, both from the CityU of Hong Kong. This project consists on a series of prints and videos that represent visually the mathematical idea of approximation.

You will find below a couple of videos of the project.

Visit the web of Approximation Theory at concept-script.com/approxTheory/.
