Accomodation

We have blocked 40 rooms in the NH Hotel Berlin Mitte (Leipziger Str. 106-111, 10117 Berlin) which can be booked until the 1st of July. The conference location can be reached from there by a 15 minutes walk. The price for one person, including breakfast, is 109 euro per night and for two people, including breakfast, is 134 euro per night. For making a reservation, please make a reservation at this link. The reservations will be on a first come first serve basis. For any issues contact Mrs. Schmalle (groups.nhcollectionberlinmitte@nh-hotels.com) providing her with the MB-number MB0001680487 and the workshop title “Complexity of numerical computation”.

Of course, participants may look for other options regarding accommodation.
