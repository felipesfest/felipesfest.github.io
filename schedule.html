Schedule

The talks will take place at the Einstein-Saal of the Berlin-Brandenburg Academy of Sciences and Humanities, except the plenary talk by Felipe Cucker and the talks by Héctor Rodríguez and by Jean-Pierre Bourguignon that will take place at the room MA004 of the the Institut für Mathematik of the Technische Universität Berlin. More information in the venue page.

The dinner will take place at the Restaurant “San Marino” situated at Savignyplatz 12, 10623 Berlin. One can walk there in approximately 30 minutes from the Technische Universität Berlin. It is possible to attend the dinner only if one has registered for it. More information about the restaurant at this link.
